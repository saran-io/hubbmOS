    THE ESSENTIALS

    Automation Intelligence:
    Advanced Analytics, Purpose-Built for Automation

    To truly take control, you don’t need more monitoring; you need intelligence.

    Automation Intelligence provides you with observability and so much more. It provides a unified view of your entire automation landscape, along with advanced analytics that enable you to analyze, predict, and optimize for business outcomes.

    What is automation observability?

    Automation environments are extremely complex. At any given organization, there are often multiple automation tools and multiple vendors, with hundreds of thousands — if not millions — of individual jobs running every week. It’s essential to gain unified visibility and proactively manage your workload to keep critical processes humming happily.

    What is SLA management for automation?

    Regardless of industry, automation tools play a critical role in business success. Service Level Agreements are often put in place to ensure that the business runs smoothly, maximizes its profits, and satisfies its customers.

    Why is SLA management important for automation?

    By defining and monitoring SLAs for automated business processes, organizations gain the ability to measure and analyze past performance; monitor, alert, and act on today’s performance insights; and forecast future performance to minimize risk.

    Why do you need a purpose-built solution for automation analytics?

    While tools like Splunk and Tableau provide data analysis and visualization, they have limited real-time monitoring, lack seamless integration, and limit proactive interaction with live data from enterprise automation solutions. AAI is a purpose-built solution for workload visualization and optimization with advanced SLA management and predictive analytics.
