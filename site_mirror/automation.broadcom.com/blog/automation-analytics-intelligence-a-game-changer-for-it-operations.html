    Automation Analytics & Intelligence: A Game-Changer for IT Operations

    Employ Automation Analytics & Intelligence (AAI) to take control. Establish predictive insights, proactive governance, and enterprise-wide visibility.

    Tony Beeston

    3 min read | February 10, 2025

    Highlights

    • Leverage Automation Analytics & Intelligence (AAI) and take control of your entire automation landscape.
    • Gain true, enterprise-wide visibility that delivers predictive insights and enables proactive governance.
    • Access new, intuitive visualizations that help teams quickly detect patterns, monitor SLA adherence, and prioritize areas needing immediate attention.

     

    Broadcom’s Automation Analytics & Intelligence (AAI) is the only analytics and SLA governance solution that provides true end-to-end visibility and predictive insights across your workload automation platforms, including Automic, AutoSys, CA-7, Control-M, ESP, IWS/IWA, Tidal and Airflow. Whether you run these products individually or you have a mix of solutions, with AAI, you can take control of your entire automation landscape. AAI enables you to break down silos, make data-driven decisions, and prevent disruptions.

    Here’s what makes AAI a must-have solution for enterprises:

    • 60% reduction in SLA breaches—Customers leveraging AAI have dramatically improved their ability to meet critical business metrics (SLAs), preventing costly business delays and improving customer satisfaction.
    • Millions in efficiency gains—By eliminating manual monitoring, reducing downtime, and ensuring timely delivery of business-critical data, AAI has helped organizations unlock multi-million-dollar productivity improvements.
    • Predictive analytics—AAI continuously analyzes your automation landscape, alerting teams to potential failures before they have an impact on your business.
    • Cross-scheduler visibility—Unlike other solutions that lock customers into a single platform, AAI seamlessly integrates with many major workload automation tools, ensuring a unified, holistic view of automation performance across distributed and mainframe environments.
    • Smart SLA governance—No more chasing after missed SLAs. AAI proactively checks that jobs will complete on time, aligning automation execution with business priorities.

    Recent innovation

    The latest release of AAI introduces Business Area Monthly Performance, a visual heat map that highlights service delivery trends over the past 12 months. At a glance, users can identify:

    • Red zones: Months where SLA failures occurred, signaling performance issues.
    • Green zones: Months with SLA successes, showcasing operational efficiency and reliability.


    Business Area Monthly Performance

    This intuitive visualization helps teams quickly detect patterns, monitor SLA adherence, and prioritize areas needing immediate attention. These views also provide a great way to share the results of automation with all stakeholders.

    Beyond high-level insights, AAI allows users to drill down into specific business areas and processes, and uncover the root causes of performance issues. With AAI, teams can:

    • Pinpoint specific periods with service delivery failures.
    • Identify underperforming sub-business areas and processes affecting overall service delivery.
    • Understand recurring challenges and their root causes.

    Why settle for less?

    If you’re still relying on manual intervention, fragmented tools, or gut feeling to manage workload automation, you're already falling behind. With business demands accelerating and IT complexity growing, enterprises need intelligent automation insights to stay competitive.

    Organizations across industries—from financial services to manufacturing—are already using AAI to revolutionize their automation. The question is: Are you ready to join them?
