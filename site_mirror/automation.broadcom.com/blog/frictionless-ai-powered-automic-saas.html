    Innovate Faster: Automic SaaS Redefines Automation with a Frictionless, AI-Powered Experience

    Kaj Wierda

    4 min read | October 8, 2025

    Highlights

    • Find out about the key capabilities introduced in the latest release of Automic SaaS.
    • Harness the solution’s frictionless orchestration to seamlessly connect enterprise workflows to cloud services.
    • Employ a new Code Assist feature, which makes development faster, more accessible, and less error prone.

    In a world that demands constant innovation, maximizing the speed and agility of your IT operations is more critical than ever. To support you in these aims, we are thrilled to announce the latest release of Automic SaaS, 24.6, which represents a significant step forward in empowering IT professionals to drive digital transformation. This release introduces two powerful new capabilities: a frictionless, agentless orchestration model and a generative AI-powered Code Assist feature. These capabilities are designed to help you focus on what truly matters: delivering value to your business.

    Focus on automation, not administration, with a frictionless orchestration experience

    Imagine a world where you can seamlessly connect your enterprise workflows to critical cloud services—without the administrative burden of installing, configuring, or managing agents. With our new frictionless orchestration experience, that world is now a reality.

    We are fundamentally changing how you integrate with key services like SAP, cloud storage, and REST endpoints. By hosting and managing the integration infrastructure within our SaaS platform, we have removed a major hurdle to innovation. This "just run" approach is a direct response to the needs of our customers, particularly those embracing cloud-first strategies like RISE with SAP, who require seamless automation in managed environments.

    For your teams, this means a dramatic reduction in the time it takes to get new automation projects up and running. Line-of-business users and application teams can now browse and directly use available integrations, significantly reducing dependencies on central IT and administrative teams. This simplified model not only accelerates your time to value but also paves the way for a broader set of cloud and data pipeline services, empowering you to tackle more cloud-centric use cases with ease.

    Your intelligent scripting partner: GenAI-powered Code Assist

    We are embedding the power of GenAI directly into the Automic script editor. Our new Code Assist feature acts as an intelligent partner, making automation development faster, more accessible, and less error prone.

    This is a game-changer for citizen developers and subject matter experts, who can now translate their deep process knowledge into robust automation, without needing to be scripting gurus. Simply describe your goal in plain English, and the AI assistant will generate the necessary Python, Bash, or PowerShell code.

    But the Code Assist is not just for new scripts. It can also analyze and enhance your existing code. Ask it to explain a complex section, suggest optimizations for efficiency, add robust error handling, or refactor for greater clarity. By augmenting the capabilities of your team, you can reduce development cycles, improve the quality and resilience of your automation, and scale your organization’s automation initiatives more effectively than ever before.

    Looking ahead

    The Automic SaaS 24.6 release is more than just an update; it's a new way of thinking about enterprise automation. By removing friction and embedding intelligence, we are empowering you to increase business agility, lower operational costs, and enable a broader set of users to build the automation that drives your business forward.

    We are incredibly excited to bring these new capabilities to our SaaS customers in early October and look forward to seeing the innovative solutions you will build.
