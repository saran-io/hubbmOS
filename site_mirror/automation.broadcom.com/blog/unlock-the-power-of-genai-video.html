    Automic Automation 24.4: Unlock the Power of GenAI

    Automic Automation 24.4 brings GenAI automation, letting you summarize reports, troubleshoot faster, and embed AI in scripts to boost efficiency.

    April 30, 2025

    Video Transcript

    Automic Automation 24.4 introduces integrated generative AI capabilities, embedding the power of LLMs directly in the Automic web interface with support for models such as Gemini, GPT, or those running locally via Olama. Automic empowers you to integrate an AI model of your choice to unlock a host of capabilities: summarize execution results and reports, troubleshoot faster, use natural language queries in any language, and even embed generative AI capabilities into your scripts.

    Traditionally, diagnosing execution failures required manual inspection of jobs, workflows, scripts, logs, and other objects. Now, you just have to right click on any executable object to trigger an AI based analysis directly within the UI. Whether it's a failed job, unresponsive agent, or misconfigured workflow, GenAI analyzes associated logs, reports, and execution data, and surfaces root causes in clear, structured summaries.

    Summarizing long reports would consume time and reduce efficiency. Now, GenAI analyzes job reports and generates summaries highlighting alerts, performance issues, or failures for rapid triage. If a job enters an unexpected state, GenAI identifies the error and explains why it occurred, whether due to missing permissions, a runtime condition, or misconfiguration.

    It doesn't stop with summaries. GenAI explains complex script logic and suggests improvements. From failed jobs to connection timeouts, GenAI reduces mean time to resolution by providing actionable suggestions. You can even ask the AI assistant suggestions for improvement in your language of preference. GenAI understands the query and responds conversationally.

    Automic Automation 24.4 introduces a powerful new GenAI scripting function called "Ask AI." With this function, you can embed AI queries directly into your scripts, invoking the assistant at runtime to analyze aspects of execution data, outputs, or errors. For example, you can use GenAI to assess the error severity and assign priority levels to them based on the error message context, helping efficient integration with ticketing systems.

    Empower every user, deepen operational visibility, and catch potential issues before they occur. Transform your automation processes with Automic Automation 24.4. Experience the future of automation with the power of GenAI.
