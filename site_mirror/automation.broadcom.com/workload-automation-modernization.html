    Benefits of Modern Automation Platforms

    Embracing a modern Service Orchestration and Automation Platform is a transformational move that fuels innovation and makes automation a strategic advantage for your business.

    Connect and consolidate your existing data and automation tools

    Unified automation solutions like Broadcom’s Automic Automation and AutoSys eliminate data silos by providing centralized control and self-service workflows. Scale your automation strategy without losing the benefits of the cloud tools you love.

    Broadcom’s Automation Marketplace has hundreds of pre-built plugins and connectors that make it easy to integrate tools you already use, including ServiceNow, Oracle, SAP, Google Cloud, AWS and many more.

    Leverage predictive analytics to navigate change with confidence

    Advanced analytics capabilities purpose-built for workload automation like Broadcom’s Automation Analytics & Intelligence (AAI) use predictive analytics to provide service-level assurance, critical path management, and enterprise-wide observability. With AAI you can anticipate future-state challenges and successfully manage complex workloads across multiple scheduling solutions.

    Tame the complexity of SLA management

    SLA management provides business service insight from workload automation activities across all vendors and platforms, with predictive alerting, self-service dashboards, and integrated audit and compliance reporting. Understand and monitor the delivery of your SLAs to reduce the risk of breaches and fines.

    GenAI technologies can significantly scale automation through natural language interfaces, predictive analytics, and self-healing workflows. Modern automation solutions also play an essential role in augmenting GenAI initiatives by orchestrating the supply of clean and compliant data. This symbiotic relationship signifies the beginning of an automation renaissance.
