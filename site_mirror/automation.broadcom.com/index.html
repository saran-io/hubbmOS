    Navigating today’s complex and fragmented automation landscape

    Automation is vital for running core business operations, driving efficiency, and boosting productivity in enterprises. However, in today’s hybrid cloud environments, automation becomes fragmented and disconnected, leading to missed SLAs and less-than-optimal service delivery.

    Hybrid Cloud Driving Fragmentation

    We operate in a hybrid, multi-cloud environment with fragmented teams and tools, and the complexity is only expected to grow. Relying on isolated automated processes will amplify inefficiencies and isn't sustainable.

    Self-Service Automation Requirements

    Business and application teams require the flexibility of self-managed automated processes with access to the best practices of workload teams. Self-service improves time to value, but leads to more automation tools and more complexity.

    Acceleration is on the Horizon

    If enterprises want to realize the promise of digital transformation and remain competitive, automation needs to scale at the speed of modernization in a sustainable way—and modern solutions need to empower teams to automate more. For this, GenAI is the essential enabler.
